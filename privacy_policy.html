Privacy Policy

This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for Sipnetic application offered by IPTON.

Data collection

We do not collect any of your personal data.

In-app purchases

When you make an in-app purchase you interact with Google Billing API. This is all done through your Google account. We do not have access to your credit card details or any other payment information. We only receive a confirmation about the purchase.

User reviews

In the case of user reviews on Google Play, data you have consented to share with Google is shared with Google only. We do not have access to personal data, outside of the display name you have consented to sharing publicly when publishing a review. You may receive a reply to a review which is sent to you by email from Google, however your email address itself is not revealed, stored or in any other way processed by IPTON.

Crash reporting

In the case of crash reporting, some information concerning the device is visible, such as make and model. All data is non-personal and cannot be used to identify you or contact you.
